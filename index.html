RE: LAUNCH OF THE 2026 USEAA ADMISSION COMPETITIVE TEST (USAT)

Enclosure 1 — Admission Process

  • S-Registration: Create an account online. Receive S-Register Number (26-#####) via email.
  • S-Application: Login and complete the form. Settle payment or apply for waiver online.
  • Approval: Receive Application ID (AID#####) upon approval.
  • USAT Exam: Choose exam date and venue. Slot is FINAL upon confirmation.
  • Results: Released 2 months after the exam date.

Enclosure 2 — USAT Exam

  • Subjects: Language Proficiency, Mathematics, Science, Reading Comprehension
  • Items: 100 items per subject (400 total)
  • LP: 45 minutes | Math: 1.5 hrs
  • Science: 1 hr 20 min | RC: 1 hr
  • Scoring: +1 correct, −0.25/−0.50/−1.00 wrong (progressive), 0 unanswered
  • Score: Released as Normalized Performance Percentage (NPP)

Enclosure 4 — Results & Appeals

  • Passer — Met cut-off and secured a program slot
  • Waitlisted — Met cut-off but no available slot
  • DPWAS — Met cut-off, offered a different program
  • Rejected — Did not meet cut-off

Maximum of 4 appeal cycles. Appeals are strictly ACI rank-based — no first-appeal-first-slot priority. Waitlisted/DPWAS are given priority in all appeal rounds unless they waive their status.
